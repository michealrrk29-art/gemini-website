Why Gemini

Sourcing from the plant, not the market

Direct factory & FAB contracts

We hold long-term direct agreements with manufacturers and foundries. Supply moves on those contracts — not through grey-market channels or layered chains of intermediaries — which is what makes pricing, volume, and continuity dependable.

Authenticity & traceability

Every shipment is traceable to the producing plant, with certificates of origin and the documentary chain to evidence it. Sourcing at origin removes the counterfeit and re-marking risk that defines the open component market.

Cross-border settlement & logistics

We structure payment and delivery across the jurisdictions where conventional channels are constrained — the same multi-currency settlement and logistics capability that underpins the rest of our practice, applied to component supply.
