Tanker-class wheat shipment, 30,000 metric tonnes

Acted on the structuring and settlement side of a Black Sea origin wheat shipment to an Iraqi state-aligned buyer. Coordinated documentary, logistical, and payment architecture across three jurisdictions.

Concurrent direct working dialogue with PV Gas (Vietnam) and major Russian gas exporters on long-term cooperation frameworks.

Practice areas

What we do

Transaction structuring

Multi-jurisdictional sale-and-purchase architectures for physical commodities and capital goods, with particular focus on cases where direct banking is constrained. We build the legal, financial, and logistical structure of the deal end-to-end.

Settlement engineering

Multi-currency, multi-bank settlement chains designed around the operational realities of our clients' jurisdictions — including national-currency, dirham, yuan, and gold-backed structures.

Sovereign & quasi-sovereign mandates

Origination and execution of transactions involving state buyers, state sellers, and state-aligned counterparts. Particular depth in West and East Africa and Southeast Asia.

Commodities trade

Physical trade, brokerage, and structuring across precious metals, grain, fertilisers, oil products, and natural gas / LNG.

Special situations

Claim resolution, pre-closing restructuring, and cross-border dispute mediation where commercial logic still permits a transaction.

Russia–Africa trade settlement: structural reality of 2025

Over 84% of Russia–Africa transactions now bypass the dollar and euro. The shift is real, but the structural ceiling — Russia's $27B trade footprint vs China's $150B — sets the limits of the corridor.

Trade with India: why the rupee channel never worked

Despite political momentum, rupee settlements never reached operational scale. The corridor has migrated to dirham via the UAE and yuan via mainland China.
